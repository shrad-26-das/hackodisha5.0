ğŸŒ¿ Simple Yoga Poses

Gentle stretches to release tension

Child's Pose

Kneel and sit back on your heels. Stretch arms forward, lowering forehead to the ground. Hold for 30 seconds.

Cat-Cow Stretch

Start on hands and knees. Arch your back (cow), then round it (cat). Repeat 5-10 times slowly.

Mountain Pose

Stand tall with feet hip-width apart. Arms at sides, breathe deeply. Focus on grounding and balance.

Legs Up Wall

Lie on your back near a wall. Extend legs up the wall. Relax arms by your sides for 5-10 minutes.

Seated Twist

Sit cross-legged. Place right hand behind you, left hand on right knee. Gently twist. Repeat other side.

Forward Fold

Stand with feet hip-width apart. Slowly bend forward, letting arms hang loose. Hold for 30 seconds.
